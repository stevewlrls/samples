Dracula!

Chapter One: Arrival in Transylvania

It is a dark and stormy night. Up on the hilltop, the jagged lightning reveals a massive castle. On the road leading up to it, a black coach is being drawn by four horses, their hooves clattering on the stones and throwing sparks from their iron shoes.

As the coach gets close to the castle, a massive gate opens in two halves, allowing the horse to clatter through to a courtyard in front of the keep. There is no coachman to drive the coach, but the horses seem to know what they are doing. They slow down as they enter the castle, and the coach comes to a stop right by the steps to the main door.
